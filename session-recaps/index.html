Session 1

And so it Begins

April 17, 2026

The party woke outside the Moss Woods alongside runners William and Matty, both marked by scars from a recent fight near the Anchorpine Forest. They carried payment of 16 silver and a message from Millwright, tied to Lady Alexa Provast and her operations out of The Mill.

The group was directed to a dead drop in Eiremoss (Telton St.) for their next job. Entering through the Southern Gate, they noted a heavier guard presence than usual but passed without issue, securing their mounts at The Mill before heading to the drop.

At the dead drop, they found three children and two guards, one distracted by a street con. Umashi noticed someone watching from a nearby window who quickly withdrew upon being seen. Using a coordinated distraction, the party retrieved the drop unnoticed.

The new job directed them to Drausvine Hospital, where they were to assist Dr. Stret, who owed Millwright, by extracting live bodies from the morgue.

Before moving on the hospital, Thimble and Eldric stopped at The Mill, meeting Dribble, Martin, and The Pelt. From them, the party acquired four blades, confirming that more than one individual would need to be rescued. With preparation complete, they regrouped with Zeharith and Umashi, who had begun scouting the hospital.

Reconnaissance revealed Drausvine Hospital to be relatively quiet, with two entrances, lighted paths, and roof access via lattice. Zeharith’s familiar, Mittens, scouted inside, revealing rooms filled with dead or near-dead patients, a gated stairwell leading downward, and active morgue work in the southern section, with only a single Orderly moving throughout the building.

Inside, the party encountered Jane, a well-read clerk who noted that Dr. Stret had been locked in his office for hours and that visitors typically arrived later at night. The Orderly, Peter, revealed himself as their inside contact, warning them they had arrived too early and expressing concern not about Stret, but about Dr. Wembly. After being bribed with 4 silver, Peter agreed to distract Jane.

Using the distraction, Thimble infiltrated the office from the roof and discovered Dr. Stret unconscious on the floor with visible strangulation marks, indicating he had been attacked.

In the room, she found a potion of healing, a potion of gaseous form, a runed severed hand preserved in a jar that appeared experimental in nature, and a notebook detailing research into a cure for Mind Rot, along with dangerous notes involving Dr. Wembly. Thimble left a message for Stret reading “We’re Even,” signed Provast.

While inside the office, Thimble was attacked by a separate animated hand creature, distinct from the runed hand in the jar. This hostile entity is believed to be the same type of creature that incapacitated Dr. Stret. Thimble destroyed it.

As this unfolded, a wagon arrived carrying live cargo, unconscious bodies that were brought inside and locked in storage. Peter then descended to the lower level and secured it behind him. Shortly after, Jane discovered Stret’s condition and panicked, prompting the party to regroup.

Eldric and Thimble worked to calm Jane, while Zeharith and Umashi moved to assess the stored bodies. With the information gathered, it became clear that Eldric and Zeharith could likely complete Stret’s research and finalize a cure for Mind Rot.

Jane, now fearful, insisted that she and Stret needed to flee the city and provided the party with a safe key, warning them not to open Dr. Wembly’s door without protection.

The group resolved to move Jane and Stret to The Mill for safety, while preparing for whatever waited below the morgue and within Wembly’s domain.
